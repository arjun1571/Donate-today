Our Donate Plan

The money you give is where we help and how we do it.

Food for a family

Our Dunet Group works to deliver food to the poorest of the poor. We ensure food for people affected by floods and natural disasters so that no one goes hungry.

Shelter for a family

There are hundreds of people in our country who have lost their homes due to floods and natural calamities. Our group gave them a little space to stay. Everyone has the right to life.

Education for a child

Learning is the key to the progress of a nation. It is our pledge to ensure the education of poor children so that their lives do not go to the abyss without education.
